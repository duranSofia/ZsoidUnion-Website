Planetary Highlights

Encounters that humans have never even imagined.
Discover how we live and how us Aliens have fun in our planets. Check out our top experiences and book your intergalactic visit.

Radioactive Dunes

A thrilling adventure in the most visited dunes of our galaxy, explore our territory but be careful for the mutated creatures from the Red Dunes. This tour includes the Red Zorpx Dunes; GorqL Canyon and nuclear ponds.

Nova Ship

The rescued race from KporeQ planet have lived in this ship since the destruction of the atmosphere that made the planet uninhabitable. A young race affected by their internal combats that caused destruction of the planet.

Worlorn Underworld Dive

Humans have never dive so deep, so far and so close to the habitants of an underwater world as the one in Worlorn. Dip into this world and discover a unique aquatic trip. You will share your capsule with Alien hosts and enjoy Worlorn style dinners.

Cryo Colony

Superhumans is it possible? The Cryo Colony has invented time travel, have two hearts and regerating skin. This species wants to share with humans advanced technologies and methods to rescue the a weakened race in Earth from its extintion.

Camp at Zorpx Sands

Join the Grulis and spend time with them in their camp site. Enjoy the skyline and the five moons of the planet Zorpx in the Alpha Quadrant. The Grulis are immortal energy beings with vast powers that can transfer energy to the humankind.

Rocket Ultraspeed Race

An adrenaline rush like no other! Forget about gravity and physics, in this event all human known rules dissapear. Train with Shakri experts about rocket mastery to survive the race.

Black Hole Immersion

Travel into another dimension, this unknown gateway for humans where matter is so dense it sucks in even light particles. The passage is a mystery, are you ready for it?

Glow Warp Milkyway

It is a dream of many humans to experience the night glow. Now you enjoy this view from outer space, a shift of perspective in this magical phenomenon. Its Glow season in Mars too, we'll take you to explore a radiant red planet.
